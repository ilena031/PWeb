How to Join a Class

Getting started takes less than 5 minutes. Here's all you need to do:

01
🔍

Browse & Pick a Course

Choose the class that suits your level and schedule. We offer weekday, weekend, and evening options.

02
📝

Fill in the Form

Click "Enroll Now", fill in your name, contact, and which course you'd like to join.

03
💳

Complete Payment

Pay via bank transfer or e-wallet. Your spot is confirmed once payment is received.

04
📩

Get Your Confirmation

Receive a WhatsApp confirmation with class details, address, and what to bring (just yourself!).

05
🧑‍🍳

Show Up & Bake!

Arrive 10 minutes early, put on your apron, and get ready for the most delicious class of your life.
